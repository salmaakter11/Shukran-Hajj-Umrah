Bangladeshi citizens' requirements for Hajj and Umrah visas

As a citizen of Bangladesh, you will be ready to apply for an Umrah visa if you meet these requirements. To make sure you have the most recent information and instructions for a seamless visa application process, it is always advised to speak with the appropriate authorities or a reputable Hajj & Umrah Association.

As a citizen of Bangladesh, you have to fulfil certain requirements in order to be granted an Umrah visa. The following are the essential requirements for this:

  • Valid Passport
  • COVID-19 immunisation records are necessary.
  • Completed Visa Application Form
  • Recent Passport-Sized Photographs
  • Vaccination Certificate
  • Two copies of current PP-size photos
  • valid passport with a six-month expiration date
  • NID Card
  • Birth Certificate Copy
  • Marriage Certificate Copy (for Couples)
